NAME
       mv - move or rename files

SYNOPSIS
       mv SOURCE DEST

DESCRIPTION
       Move or rename SOURCE to DEST.

EXAMPLES
       Rename a file:

           mv old.txt new.txt

       Move to another directory:

           mv file.txt /home/user/documents/

SEE ALSO
       cp(1), rm(1)
