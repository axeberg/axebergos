NAME
       bg - continue job in background

SYNOPSIS
       bg [JOB]

DESCRIPTION
       Resume  a stopped job and run it in the background. If no JOB is speci-
       fied, the current job (marked with + in jobs output) is used.

ARGUMENTS
       JOB
           Job specification. Can be:
           o   %N - Job number N
           o   %+ or %% - Current job
           o   %- - Previous job
           o   %string - Job whose command begins with string


OPTIONS
       -h, --help
           Display usage information and exit.

EXAMPLES
       Continue current job in background:

           bg

       Continue job 2 in background:

           bg %2

SEE ALSO
       fg(1), jobs(1), kill(1)
