[1mNAME[0m
       xxd - make a hexdump

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
       [1mxxd [22m[[4mFILE[24m]

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
       Create a hex dump of a file or standard input. Output shows offset, hex
       bytes, and ASCII representation.

[1mOPTIONS[0m
       [1m-h[22m, [1m--help[0m
           Display usage information and exit.

[1mOUTPUT FORMAT[0m
       Each line contains:
       â€¢   8-character hex offset
       â€¢   Up to 16 bytes in hexadecimal (grouped in pairs)
       â€¢   ASCII representation (non-printable as '.')


[1mEXAMPLES[0m
       Hex dump a file:

           xxd file.txt

       Hex dump from stdin:

           echo "Hello" | xxd
