[1mNAME[0m

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
       [1mfree [22m[[1m-h[22m]

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
       Display  the  total amount of free and used physical memory in the sys‚Äê
       tem, including shared memory usage.

[1mOPTIONS[0m
       [1m-h[22m, [1m--human[0m
           Print sizes in human readable format (e.g., 1K, 234M, 2G).

       [1m--help[0m

[1mOUTPUT COLUMNS[0m
